Privacy Policy

Welcome to CureValue!

We are deeply committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is managed with the utmost care and responsibility. This Privacy Policy provides a comprehensive overview of how we gather, use, disclose, and protect your data when you visit our website, located at https://curevalue.org, along with any associated media forms, channels, or mobile websites (collectively referred to as the “Site”).

Please read this Privacy Policy thoroughly. If you disagree with any part of this policy, we advise you not to access the Site.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

We collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us. This includes details such as your name, shipping address, email address, and telephone number, as well as demographic data like your age, gender, hometown, and interests. This information is collected when you register on the Site or participate in various activities such as online chats and message boards. While you are not obligated to provide any personal information, choosing not to may limit your access to certain features of the Site.

Derivative Data

Our servers automatically collect certain information when you access the Site. This includes data like your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you visited before and after accessing our Site. This data helps us understand user behavior and improve the overall experience.

Financial Data

We may collect financial information, such as payment method details (e.g., credit card number, card brand, expiration date), when you make purchases, orders, returns, exchanges, or inquiries about our services. We store only minimal financial information directly. Most financial data is stored by our payment processors, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies and reach out to them directly with any questions.

Use of Your Information

The information we collect enables us to offer you a seamless, efficient, and tailored experience. Specifically, we may use your data to:

  1. Account Management: Create and manage your user account on the Site.
  2. Targeted Communication: Send you targeted advertising, coupons, newsletters, and other promotional materials.
  3. Order Fulfillment: Process and manage your purchases, orders, payments, and related transactions.
  4. Personalization: Generate a personal profile to make future visits to the Site more personalized and relevant.
  5. Site Optimization: Enhance the efficiency and functionality of the Site.
  6. Analytics: Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience.
  7. Updates and Notifications: Inform you about updates to the Site, new products, services, mobile applications, and recommendations.
  8. Business Activities: Conduct other business activities as necessary.

We employ administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information.
